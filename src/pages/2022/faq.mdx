## General Conference
  
### When is the 2022 Annual Conference?
  
November 10-13, 2022

### I have a question which is not answered here.

 - Logistics (travel, non-sponsor registration): conference-logistics@ostem.org

### How is COVID-19 affecting the conference?

We have decided to host our 2022 conference to in person. Our COVID-19 mitigation strategy is still under development; more details will be forthcoming.

## Registration

### How do I register for the conference?

Registration for the conference will open later this summer.

### How much does registration cost?
  
 - Students
   - $150 members,
   - $225 non-members,
 - Expo Only
   - $75 one day ticket
   - $100 2-day ticket
 - Professional
   - $425 member
   - $525 non-member
  
### What does conference registration cover?

- The Student and Professional tickets are both all-access passes to conference programming, events, and meals.
  - oSTEM will provide Friday lunch and dinner and Saturday breakfast, lunch, and dinner. There is no general Sunday breakfast or lunch this year.
- The Expo-only pass grants access only to the Graduate School and Career Fair Expo on Friday, November 11 and Saturday, November 12.

### Will there be on-site registration?
  
No.
