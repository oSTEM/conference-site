**oSTEM Collegiate Conference Grant Applications for 2025**

The Collegiate Conference Grant is used to help chapters/individuals fund their trip to the Annual oSTEM Conference in Baltimore, MD. This year, funds will be disbursed by means of:
- Conference Registration Codes (which will be used to cover the cost of granted conference registrations per chapter)
- Lodging Accommodations (hotel rooms)

Huge thank you to our sponsors for making this Collegiate Conference Grant possible!

This year, the application is split into individual or chapter applications. Students are encouraged to apply based on the circumstance that best fits their situation.

You may apply for a Conference Grant at the following link: https://form.jotform.com/251597266915166

**The deadline to submit your application is Friday, September 5th, 2025 at 11:59pm PT. Please take note of the time zone.**

We expect decisions to be released by Sunday, September 14, 2025 at the latest.

Please direct any questions to our Co-Directors of Collegiate Membership at membership@ostem.org. Thank you.
