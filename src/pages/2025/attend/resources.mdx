## General Resources

</div>

We are using an online platform called Whova for our conference this year. All conference attendees have access to Whova, both as an app on your phone and as a web app on your laptop. To enter Whova, sign in using the **same** email you used in registration and the oSTEM 2025 event will show up!

Things you can do in Whova include but are not limited to:
- Viewing the event agenda and planning your personal schedule
- Accessing documents and slides shared by organizers or speakers
- Finding others this event and planning or joining them in social activities such as a morning run, coffee hours, or meet-ups with your fellow attendees
- (Mobile) Receiving updates such as announcements and  last minute session changes from the organizers

## Getting to the Conference

</div>

The 15<sup>th</sup> annual conference will be taking place at the <a href='https://www.bccenter.org/' target='_blank'>Baltimore Convention Center</a> in Baltimore, MD.

The address of the Convention Center is <b>1 West Pratt Street, Baltimore, MD 21201</b>.

<br/>

<div className='flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-x-4'>
<div className='w-full'>
<h3 className='text-green-900 dark:text-green-100'><FontAwesomeIcon icon={faPlane} className={"social-icon"} fixedWidth /> Plane</h3>

The closest airport is Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport (BWI). The [MTA  Light RailLink (Light Rail)](https://www.mta.maryland.gov/schedule/lightrail) offers a direct connection from the airport to the Convention Center and costs $2.00 each way.

## Lodging

</div>

### General Lodging Information

Please see the hotel blocks below for the 2025 Annual Conference. These hotels have discounted group rates available exclusively for attendees, and are all located close to both the Light Rail station and the Convention Center. Because we have multiple hotels this year, please note the following:

- There are three general hotels that can be reserved by any attendee.
  - All hotels are within 5 blocks of both the Convention Center and the Light Rail station.
- Hotel blocks are first come, first served.
  - This page will be updated weekly to indicate remaining capacity at each hotel. Once a hotel is full, you will not be able to make any reservations under our block.
  - Reservations are available for booking Tuesday, October 15th through Monday, October 21st. Please note that Tuesday, Wednesday, and Sunday night availability is limited.

## Conference Accessibility

</div>
For general accessibility questions, please email our accessibility team at <a href="mailto:access@ostem.org">access@ostem.org</a>.

During online registration we ask what accommodations you need. We aim to fulfill all reasonable accommodations requested by **August 31st**; after that date, we will do what we can but our ability to fulfill accommodations may be more limited. You can also email us for inquiries into accommodations.

For additional accessibility information available at the convention center, please visit https://www.bccenter.org/p/about/faqs.
