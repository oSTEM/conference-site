## Getting to the Conference

</div>

The 14<sup>th</sup> annual conference will be taking place at the <a href='https://www.oregoncc.org/en' target='_blank'>Oregon Convention Center</a> in Portland, OR.

The address of the convention center is <b>777 NE Martin Luther King, Jr. Boulevard, Portland, OR 97232</b>.

<br/>

<div className='flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-x-4'>
<div className='w-full'>
<h3 className='text-green-900 dark:text-green-100'><FontAwesomeIcon icon={faPlane} className={"social-icon"} fixedWidth /> Plane</h3>

The closest airport is Portland International Airport (PDX). The [TriMet MAX Red Line (light rail)](https://trimet.org/home/planner-trip?fromPlace=Portland%20Int%27l%20Airport%20MAX%20Station%20(ID%2010579)%3A%3A45.587584%2C-122.5931&toPlace=Convention%20Center%20MAX%20Station%3A%3A45.5300519305776%2C-122.661907990664&date=2024-06-09&time=17%3A21&arriveBy=false&mode=WALK%2CBUS%2CTRAM%2CRAIL%2CGONDOLA&showIntermediateStops=true&maxWalkDistance=1609&walkSpeed=1.34&ignoreRealtimeUpdates=false&numItineraries=1&otherThanPreferredRoutesPenalty=900&tripOption=1) offers a direct connection from the airport to the convention center and costs $2.80 each way.
</div>
<div className='w-full'>
<h3 className='text-green-900 dark:text-green-100'><FontAwesomeIcon icon={faTrain} className={"social-icon"} fixedWidth /> Train</h3>

The [Amtrak PDX station](https://www.amtrak.com/stations/pdx) is the closest station to the Oregon Convention Center. Similar to PDX airport, you can take the light rail directly from the train station to the convention center.

</div>
<div className='w-full'>
<h3 className='text-green-900 dark:text-green-100'><FontAwesomeIcon icon={faCar} className={"social-icon"} fixedWidth /> Car</h3>

If you're driving and need parking, please refer to [the OCC website for detailed parking information](https://www.oregoncc.org/en/attend/parking-directions). There is a charge for on-site parking which will not be covered by oSTEM.
</div>
