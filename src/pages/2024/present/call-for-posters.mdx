Out in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (oSTEM) has not yet started accepting posters for the in-person 14th Annual oSTEM Conference 2024.

The oSTEM Annual Conference Poster Session is intended for undergraduate
students, graduate students, and postdocs to showcase their research to other
conference attendees. Anyone performing academic or industry research is invited
to submit a poster for consideration.

## Poster Display and Awards

</div>

Posters will be on display to all conference attendees during the day on
Saturday. Poster submitters will be asked to remain with their poster and
explain their research to interested attendees and judges during the Career and
Graduate Fair Expo.

Awards will be given out to the highest-scoring undergraduate, graduate, and
postdoc posters!

<div className="text-bright-peach font-bold">

## F.A.Q

</div>

- **What are the recommended poster sizes?**

  The maximum poster dimensions are 48" x 36".

- **Do I need to bring my poster? Can you print it for me?**

  Unfortunately, oSTEM cannot print your poster. We advise that you travel with
  your poster.

- **How will I know if my poster was accepted for the poster session?**

  Posters will be accepted on a rolling basis and you will receive an email
  before October to tell you the outcome of your poster submission.

- **Do students participating in the poster session receive a conference registration discount?**

  Yes, students chosen to present their research during the poster sessions will receive a discount code in their confirmation email. Our team of reviewers are volunteers and as such, it may take a while before you hear back.

- **More FAQs will be put here once Call for Posters is open for 2024.**
