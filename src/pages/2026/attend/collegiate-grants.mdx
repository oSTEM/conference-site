## Scholarship Program

</div>

Since 2021, oSTEM has awarded over $500,000 in scholarships to support our members across community college, undergraduate, and graduate programs. The application for the 2026 cycle opens on April 15, 2026, and is due on May 18, 2026. Scholarship recipients will be announced by August 2026. Recipients receive a complimentary Full Conference Collegiate Registration to the 16th Annual oSTEM Conference. Complimentary registration is non-transferable; however, registration fees may be refunded if recipients purchased their registration prior to the scholarship announcement. For more information, visit https://ostem.org/page/scholarship-program.
